tb-nudge: train the classifier

Two steps: extract a training dataset from your Sent folders, then train the classifier on it. Any human corrections you've made (in the diagnostics page — the ✓/✗ buttons or the "Scan all for labeling" pass) are merged in automatically at training time. The extracted labels are heuristic, not hand-checked — spot-check a sample before trusting the model.

1. Extract dataset

Positive (needed a reply): you followed up on your own message before any reply came, or the message asks something and got a reply.
Negative (didn't need a reply): the message is itself a short acknowledgment (thanks / sounds good), or it only drew a brief closing reply.
Everything else — silence, or genuinely ambiguous — is skipped. Thread position isn't used.
