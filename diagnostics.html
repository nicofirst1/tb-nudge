tb-nudge: diagnostics & labeling

Triggers the same check the hourly alarm runs, immediately - useful for testing after a settings/model change instead of waiting. Note the runtime window here (Settings: "nudge me after X hours" / "stop looking back after Y days") is separate and usually much narrower than the 730-day window dataset.html uses for training data.

Label the whole mailbox once (to improve the model)

Sweeps every sent message and shows what the current model would decide - without firing notifications or tagging anything. Then correct the wrong ones below, and those human labels get folded into the next training run.

  1. Scan all for labeling - runs the full read-only pass (progress shown below).
  2. In the results, click "✗ Didn't need reply" on wrong nudges and "✓ Needed reply" on wrong suppressions.
  3. Re-open dataset.html, extract, and Train - your corrections are merged in automatically. (Or Download corrections.json here for node train.js.)
